About Us

AroShield is an AI-powered platform designed to promote safe and informed medication use. By combining intelligent drug interaction analysis with real-time insights, AroShield helps users understand the composition, side effects, and compatibility of their medicines. Our mission is to simplify healthcare decision-making and empower individuals to use their medications safely through intuitive technology, image recognition, and conversational AI support.
